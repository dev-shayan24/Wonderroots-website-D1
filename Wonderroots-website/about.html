About Wonder Roots

Wonder Roots is a non-profit school dedicated to providing affordable, high-quality education to people of all ages and backgrounds. We believe in inclusive, community-driven learning and are committed to expanding our curriculum to include more subjects soon.

Our core values are integrity, growth, and opportunity for all. Our team consists of 3 dedicated teachers and 2 passionate founders, working together to create a supportive and inspiring educational environment.
